Our Experience

With over 50 projects across diverse types and sizes, MCO Group has extensive experience in renewable power generation.

We have delivered projects ranging from small scale micro-hydro plants to large complex power stations across varied geographies.

Our multidisciplinary team works effectively in designing, procurement, and construction to deliver on time and within budget.

Our EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) expertise enables delivery of large-scale complex renewable, transmission, and distribution projects efficiently and reliably.

We leverage cutting-edge technology and project monitoring tools for smart and sustainable project execution.

Our successful track record is a testament to our commitment to quality, safety, and environmental standards.

The company embodies a serious work ethic with teams trained to operate in challenging conditions, including mountainous regions of the Himalayas.

Our teams have specialized skills in geotechnical assessments, environmental impact mitigation, and civil construction under difficult terrain conditions.

This makes us ideal partners for renewable projects requiring resilience and high-quality engineering.
