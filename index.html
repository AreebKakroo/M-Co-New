About Us

MCO Group has been a pioneer in renewable energy and infrastructure projects for over five decades. Our mission is to lead the transition to sustainable energy by empowering communities and industries with world-class engineering and innovation.

We specialize in hydroelectric, solar, and smart grid solutions, bringing reliable power to millions and driving economic growth worldwide.
